Accompanying Anthropology

One may be thinking..."Anthropology can't be just be about bones?"

And your exactly right!

Anthropology isn't just ONE thing, it branches into 4 subfields:

  1. Archaeology Anthropology: Study and interpretation of past societies and cultures from the beginning of times to now
  2. Cultural Anthropology: Study of origins, history, and development of human culture
  3. Biological Anthropology: Study of biological aspects of human beings, both past and present
  4. Linguistic Anthropology: Study of language

Although they all sound beyond intriguing to learn about, this page is more filled, content wise, with details about the Biological Anthropology subfield versus the other 3.

Feel free to dig into those subfields after familiarizing yourself with what content I provided within this enclypodeia.
